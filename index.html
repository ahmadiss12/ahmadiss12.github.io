Final Year Project

CoachVision — AI Fitness Coach

Full-stack coaching app that analyzes live pose landmarks, tracks movement phases, counts valid reps and holds, and gives corrective feedback mid-workout.

  • Exercise logic from 33 MediaPipe landmarks, joint-angle thresholds, and finite-state phases.
  • 12 exercises with rep counting, hold timing, posture feedback, and progress metrics.
  • Compact landmark arrays over WebSocket instead of base64 frames for a fast live pipeline.
  • Readiness logic from history, form quality, ROM trends, sleep, soreness, stress, and load.
